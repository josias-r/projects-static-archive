Silvanon Radio Play

A story about a strange being in the woods.

Silvanon was not a project I was a part of alone. Together with Frederik Flury, we created our first radio play. It was an interesting experience.

The project was not just recording a radio play, first we had to think of an idea and the characters in it, then we created a script and made sure the story was able to be told only using audio.

No visual context made it challenging to tell the story, but the topic “horror” that we have chosen made it a bit easier to realize.

The final recording:

Throughout the project we mainly worked with Adobe Audition, were we sampled all the sounds and cut everything together. We were given a professional microphone for recording.
