caddy

description:

The caddy project was, our first project to start out with the apprenticeship. We were asked to create an image representing us and our character. These images were necessary for labeling our personal lockers («Spinds»). I chose to create a digital self portrait painting using photoshop and my graphic tablet.

software used:

When you work with brushes, pen pressure, etc. and at the same time want to create something realistic you need to work with photoshop. I used some brushes from AaronGriffinArt and also some default ones.

As a reference, I used a photo that was made one year back. It has a very neutral expressions and fits the criteria needed. To finish the project, I added the necessary logo, my tdot number and arranged the background plus adding a canvas texture to imitate a real painting. In the end. the project turned out pretty well.
