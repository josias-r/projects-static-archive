naleki

description:

Our second «ZLO» was inventing a completely new product delivered as an animal food. Our instructions were creating a flyer, business card, package and of course the logo. To conclude we had to perform a presentation playing the role of someone trying to sell his product on the market.

software used:

Since this was a print product, I mostly used InDesign. First you create all the assets for your document, maybe using another software, then you put all the elements together in InDesign. I used it for the business card, the package and the flyer.

When you create a logo your are most likely going to use Illustrator for it. Because my imaginary company was only producing the product I designed, I decided to go with a lemur head as a logo, since my animal food is made for lemurs.

One part of this project was also performing a presentation as someone trying to sell the product on the market. I represented myself as the CEO and founder of Naleki and tried to convince the audience to include my product in their assortment.
