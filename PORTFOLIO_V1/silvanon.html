silvanon

description:

The third «ZLO» of the apprenticeship was to create a radio play. But it was not just about recording and editing the audio, we also needed to create the script, inventing the scenario and character, etc. It was really fun to do and my first time as a narrator.

software used:

Since this project was about creating a radio play, I of course worked with Adobe Audition. That was actually my first time ever using it and I kind of fell in love with it (like any other adobe software). There are a lot of exciting things to discover about this product and it servers it's purpose well.

InDesign was mostly just used to create an appealing script for our radioplay. It didn't have any other purposes in this project.

As you can see in the preview picture, I created some small artwork for the radio play featuring the silvanon character. I used that to decorate our script.
